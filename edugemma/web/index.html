🌐 Offline-First Architecture

EduGemma runs entirely on your device. No internet required after installation — not even for a "fallback." The AI model, the tutoring engine, and your progress data all live locally.

1

You Ask a Question

Type a STEM question, upload a photo of a textbook problem, or try a quiz — your input stays on your device.

↓
2

Gemma 4 Thinks Locally

A fine-tuned Gemma 4 E4B model processes your question via Ollama — running on your CPU/GPU. Zero cloud calls. ~2-5 second response time on any laptop.

↓
3

Adaptive Tutor Responds

Step-by-step explanations at your difficulty level, with analogies and encouragement. The engine adjusts based on your response patterns.

↓
4

Progress Saved Locally

Your mastery, quiz history, and streaks are stored in a local SQLite database. Private by default — no accounts, no cloud, no data ever leaves your device.

🧠 Model Details

Gemma 4 E4B — Mixture-of-Experts architecture with 4.5B active parameters per token (8B total). Fine-tuned on 500 curated STEM Q&A pairs using Unsloth for 2× faster training and 70% less memory. Runs on any laptop with 8GB+ RAM via Ollama.

One-command install:

curl -fsSL https://ollama.com/install.sh | sh
ollama pull edugemma:e4b
python -m uvicorn main:app
# Open http://localhost:8000 — done.
